Description

Breakfast Bunny will make your breakfast incredible, it's really simple and quickly made. It also combines super well with the Bah-Nana.
And it looks like a bunny!

Ingredients

  • 02 slices of bacon.
  • 02 eggs.
  • 01 small piece of mozzarella.
  • 01 black olive.
  • Olive oil

Steps

Let's start with our raw ingredients:

  1. Cut the mozzarella in 6 lengthy rectangles of about 6cm, they're gonna be the hair.
  2. Remove the black olive's lump.
  3. After that cut it in 2 circles, they're gonna be the eyes.

Now we can go to the cooked ingredients:

  1. Cook the bacon slices with olive oil.
  2. With the same olive oil, cook the egg in a way that it's yolk is still liquid.

Now, the final touch:

  1. Build the bunny as it is represented in the end result image.
