Description

Sausage Springs is our most famous recipe, it has over 2 hundred accesses per day! You ready?

Ingredients

  • 01 sausage.
  • 3g of brown canned beans.
  • 50mL of tomato sauce.
  • 100mL of water.
  • 1tbsp of sugar.
  • A spirit of your taste. (optional)
  • A decorative paper umbrella. (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage.
  2. Put the tomato sauce in a pan, slow cook it while putting the 100mL of water gently so it wont reduce so much.
  3. After 5 minutes it will be done, put the cooked beans on it to mix.
  4. Add the sugar and mix it well.
  5. Now you can already put it in a glass and serve. Its optional to add the spirits or the umbrella.
  6. But it's mandatory to both if you chose to add one! Hehe, have a good time!
